So whats this all about?

I love music. Maybe painfully so. I soundtrack everything. There are some strange theories that I plan on exploring here. I don't want to review and berate. I want champion the stuff I love, the things that have fallen between the cracks. I'm in now way an expert. Go get your news from Consequence of Sound nobody does it better. For my deep digs and review I find to be honest I trust The Needle Drop.

My Goal is to be the authority on Hotfire Jamz and Deep Cuts, your filter of sorts. And if I'm lucky maybe make you think about what you’re listing to instead of just consuming.
